Pre Season Checklist

  • Test Internet Connectivity – Verify a stable wired connection at each gaming station.
  • Check for 802.1x or MAB Registration – Ensure all gaming PCs/consoles are registered on the network.
  • Confirm Switch Port Availability – All required desireed ports are active.
  • Test LAN Speed – Ensure good throughput and low latency.
  • Verify DNS Resolution – Game-related domains resolve properly.

  • Update Game Clients – Rocket League, League, etc.
  • Install Required Software – Epic Games Launcher, Riot Client, etc.
  • Disable Auto-Updates During Game Time.
  • Login Test – Confirm players can log in to game accounts.

  • Headsets & Mics Working – Test mic and audio output.
  • Keyboard & Mouse Verified – Confirm inputs are functional.
  • Controller Configuration (if used) – Mapping and compatibility tested.

  • Team Roster Finalized – Starters and alternates identified.
  • Assigned Stations per Player – Labeled stations streamline matches.
  • Schedule Shared with IT/Network – Helps preempt network needs.

  • Test all games. Are you able to play all games from your location?
  • Voice Chat – Confirm Discord or in-game chat is working as desired.
  • Blocked Services Reviewed.

  • Match Reporting Tool Ready – Coaches know how to submit match results.
  • Access to Team Management Portal – Roster editing, eligibility checks, etc.
  • Contact Info for Support – Coaches know how to reach IT/CST for support.
