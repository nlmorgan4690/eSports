Pre Game Checklist

  • Verify stable internet connection on all gaming devices.
  • Reboot PCs or consoles if performance has been sluggish.
  • Ensure no OS or software updates are pending.
  • Check for consistent ping to game servers (optional).

  • Launch game client and check for updates.
  • Ensure all players are logged into their game accounts.
  • Verify correct game mode and custom match settings (if applicable).
  • Confirm region/server selection is correct.

  • Test microphones and headsets for each player.
  • Check in-game or Discord voice communication functionality.
  • Ensure proper push-to-talk or mute settings are configured.

  • Double-check that correct players are present and ready (starters).
  • Match lobby is created and invites sent (if hosting).
  • Share room code/password with opponent or verify match link.
  • Confirm match time and opponent name.

  • Mark attendance and confirm all players are eligible.
  • Remind students of sportsmanship and communication expectations.
  • Submit pre-match screenshot or form (if required by league).
